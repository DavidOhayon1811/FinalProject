Who I am.

My name is David Ohayon, I'm fifteen. I live in the south of Tel Aviv, Israel, in a neighborhood called "Neve-Tsedek". Before I lived in Israel, I lived in Paris, France, in the 11th arrondissement (district). I moved to Israel in August 2014. It's been 5 years and 9 months now that I live in Israel. Lots of French families like mine have been moving to Neve-Tsedek lately. I like Tel-Aviv more than Paris for those few but significant reasons:

  • The weather is better here and it makes the vacations way more fun than it is in Paris, it rains most of the time in that town.
  • The people are nicer and helpful, if they see someone in distress they'd help them right away and that's something that you wouldn't see in Paris, there people just dodge every person that needs help.

I study at a school named Yeshiva Bar-Ilan, it's located at 124 rothschild street, Tel-Aviv. It's a Yeshiva, which means that all students are boys and that half our school subjects are about Judaism, the other half is about physics, math, computer sciences :) etc. School in Israel is so different than where I was studying in France but in a good way.
